# Legal Content

## Source

- `lib/content/LegalContent.ts`

## Responsibility

Provides localized privacy policy and terms and conditions copy for the public legal pages. The terms content includes the clauses from the lawyer-prepared Otman AS terms document dated 5 September 2025.

## Functions

| Function | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `privacyPolicyContent` | Stores the localized privacy policy title, intro, update date, sections, and list items. |
| `termsContent` | Stores the localized terms and conditions title, intro, update date, sections, and list items. |
